About RAM Training College

Empowering the Next Generation of Healthcare Professionals

Located in Kisii, RAM Training College is a premier institution dedicated to producing competent, disciplined, and highly skilled professionals. We specialize in rigorous healthcare training with a strong emphasis on practical clinical experience and uncompromising academic excellence.

Our Mission

To provide quality service and uphold high intellectual values in all our training programs.

Our Vision

To be a leading center of excellence in healthcare training, research, and community development.
